About Us

Welcome to Samal Club Resort, where luxury meets nature. Our resort offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. With stunning views, luxurious accommodations, and top-notch amenities, we provide an unforgettable experience for our guests.

Club Samal Resort is renowned for its world-class facilities and services. Guests can indulge in a variety of leisure activities, including swimming in the crystal-clear waters of the resort's infinity pool, lounging on the sun-kissed beaches, or participating in water sports such as snorkeling, diving, and kayaking.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional hospitality and create memorable experiences for our guests, while preserving and protecting the natural beauty of our surroundings.
